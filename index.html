About

I'm a Senior Machine Learning Engineer at Instacart, where I build large-scale recommendation systems that process hundreds of millions of orders annually. My work focuses on personalized ML recommendations, intelligent product substitution algorithms, and technical infrastructure.

Previously, I developed on-device AI systems at Samsung R&D and worked on large-scale personalization at Walmart Labs. I hold an MS in Computer Science from UMass Amherst and serve as an invited reviewer for top-tier AI venues including AAAI, KDD, and ACL.
