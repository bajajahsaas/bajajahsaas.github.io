About

I'm a Senior Machine Learning Engineer II (L6) at Instacart, where I serve as ML tech lead building large-scale recommendation systems that process hundreds of millions of orders annually. My work has been featured in Instacart's shareholder letters, focusing on personalized ML recommendations that incorporate dietary needs and customer preferences, intelligent product substitution algorithms, and technical infrastructure that drives business impact measured in hundreds of millions of dollars.

With over 8 years of experience in ML and software engineering, I've delivered production systems at scale across multiple domains. My expertise spans recommendation systems, NLP, LLMs, production ML infrastructure, and applied research, with a track record of translating research into measurable business outcomes including $150M+ in retention wins.

Previously, I developed on-device AI systems at Samsung R&D handling 5M+ daily queries, and built large-scale personalization systems at Walmart Labs. I hold an MS in Computer Science from UMass Amherst, where I conducted research in NLP and machine learning.

I'm an active contributor to the research community with 127+ citations, 9 peer-reviewed publications, and 3 granted/published patents. I serve as an invited reviewer for premier AI conferences including AAAI, KDD (Applied Data Science), ACL, WWW, and UMAP, having served on 10+ technical program committees.

Beyond my work, I'm a member of the Forbes Technology Council and International Academy of Digital Arts & Sciences. I regularly speak at industry conferences and events about production ML systems, personalization, and applied AI at scale.
